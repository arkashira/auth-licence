# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Auth-Licence: Simplifying authentication and licensing for solo founders and small SaaS developers, ensuring your web applications are secure and piracy-free."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Protect your innovation with Auth-Licence: the seamless authentication and licensing solution for small developers."
2. "Empower your SaaS with Auth-Licence: the easy way to manage authentication and prevent piracy."
3. "Auth-Licence: Your partner in safeguarding web applications with effortless integration and robust licensing."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Solo SaaS Founder - Alex**
   - **Daily Job:** Developing and maintaining a web application while managing customer support and marketing.
   - **Budget:** $50/month for essential tools.
  
2. **Persona 2: Small SaaS Developer - Jamie**
   - **Daily Job:** Building features, handling customer feedback, and ensuring application security.
   - **Budget:** $100/month for tools that enhance security and user management.

3. **Persona 3: Indie Developer - Taylor**
   - **Daily Job:** Creating and launching web applications, focusing on user experience and monetization strategies.
   - **Budget:** $30/month for tools that streamline operations and protect intellectual property.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $15 per acquisition through organic traffic and lead magnets.

2. **Social Media Advertising (LinkedIn & Twitter)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $25 per acquisition through targeted ads aimed at developers and founders.

3. **Partnerships with Developer Communities (e.g., Indie Hackers, SaaS forums)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 per acquisition through referral programs and community engagement.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Auth-Licence and its benefits.
- **Week 2:** Publish a case study on the importance of authentication for small SaaS.
- **Week 3:** Create a video tutorial on integrating Auth-Licence into a web app.
- **Week 4:** Share testimonials from beta users on social media.
- **Week 5:** Write an article on common piracy threats for web applications.
- **Week 6:** Host a webinar on best practices for securing SaaS applications.
- **Week 7:** Release a comparison chart of Auth-Licence vs. competitors.
- **Week 8:** Publish a blog post on pricing strategies for SaaS products.
- **Week 9:** Share user-generated content showcasing successful integrations.
- **Week 10:** Create a newsletter summarizing key insights and updates.
- **Week 11:** Conduct a Q&A session on social media about Auth-Licence.
- **Week 12:** Release a final recap blog post highlighting the journey and user success stories.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and conduct internal testing.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Auth-Licence with promotional campaigns across channels.
- **D+30:** Collect user feedback and iterate on product features; start targeted outreach for partnerships.
- **D+90:** Analyze user engagement and sales data; prepare for the next phase of marketing based on insights.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 500 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target $10,000 MRR by D+90.
