# Business Model Canvas for auth-licence

## Customer Segments
- Solo founders and small SaaS developers looking for affordable solutions to protect their applications.
- Startups in the early stages of product development needing simple integration for authentication and licensing.
- Developers in regions with high software piracy rates, particularly in Southeast Asia.

## Value Propositions
- Easy-to-integrate authentication solutions that save time and reduce complexity for developers.
- Cost-effective licensing management to protect intellectual property and ensure revenue from legitimate users.
- Comprehensive documentation and support tailored for non-technical founders.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the product website.
- GitHub repository for open-source community engagement and contributions.
- Online developer forums and SaaS communities for targeted marketing and outreach.

## Customer Relationships
- Self-service support through detailed documentation and FAQs.
- Community-driven support via forums and GitHub issues.
- Personalized onboarding assistance for premium users.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $19/month (approx. 700 THB) for basic features, $49/month (approx. 1,800 THB) for premium features.
- One-time licensing fee for advanced integrations: $199 (approx. 7,300 THB).
- Potential revenue from affiliate partnerships with payment processors or other SaaS tools.

## Key Resources
- Development team for continuous product improvement and feature updates.
- Marketing team to drive awareness and customer acquisition.
- Technical documentation and support resources to assist users.

## Key Activities
- Product development and maintenance to ensure security and compliance.
- Marketing campaigns targeting solo founders and small SaaS developers.
- Customer support and community engagement to foster loyalty and feedback.

## Key Partners
- Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) for seamless transaction handling.
- SaaS platforms and marketplaces for potential integrations and partnerships.
- Developer communities and forums for outreach and feedback.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Salaries for developers and engineers (~$10,000/month or 360,000 THB).
- Marketing expenses: Digital marketing campaigns and community outreach (~$2,000/month or 72,000 THB).
- Customer support costs: Salaries for support staff and resources (~$3,000/month or 108,000 THB).
