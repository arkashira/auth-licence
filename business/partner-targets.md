## Partner Integration Roadmap for auth-licence

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API                  | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                          | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Stripe**                   | Free for up to $1M in transactions  | M                  | Simplifies payment processing and subscription management for SaaS apps. | Yes                      |
| **Auth0**                    | Free for up to 7,000 active users   | M                  | Provides robust authentication solutions, enhancing security for web applications. | Yes                      |
| **Firebase Authentication**   | Free for up to 10,000 monthly active users | S                  | Easy integration of user authentication, allowing developers to focus on core app features. | No                       |
| **SendGrid**                 | Free for up to 100 emails/day       | S                  | Facilitates email notifications for user authentication and licensing updates. | Yes                      |
| **Zapier**                   | Free for up to 5 Zaps               | M                  | Automates workflows between auth-licence and other apps, improving user experience. | No                       |
| **AWS Cognito**              | Free for up to 50,000 monthly active users | L                  | Scalable user authentication and management, suitable for growing SaaS businesses. | No                       |
| **Paddle**                   | Free for up to $1,000 in sales      | M                  | Offers licensing and payment solutions tailored for software products, enhancing revenue collection. | Yes                      |
| **Gumroad**                  | Free for up to $1,000 in sales      | S                  | Simplifies digital product sales and licensing for solo founders, making it easy to monetize. | Yes                      |

### Rationale
1. **Stripe**: As a leading payment processor, integrating with Stripe allows solo founders to manage subscriptions and payments seamlessly, addressing the need for payment enforcement in web applications.
  
2. **Auth0**: This integration provides a comprehensive authentication solution, ensuring that web applications are secure and user data is protected, which is critical for developers focusing on security.

3. **Firebase Authentication**: Offers an easy-to-use solution for authentication, enabling developers to implement user sign-up and login features quickly without extensive backend work.

4. **SendGrid**: Email notifications are essential for user engagement and updates regarding licensing, making SendGrid a valuable partner for enhancing communication.

5. **Zapier**: By automating workflows between auth-licence and other applications, Zapier can significantly improve the efficiency of processes for users, allowing them to connect various tools without manual intervention.

6. **AWS Cognito**: Provides a scalable solution for user management, which is essential for SaaS applications that anticipate growth and need a reliable authentication service.

7. **Paddle**: Focused on software licensing and payments, Paddle can help solo founders manage their sales and licensing processes effectively, ensuring compliance and revenue collection.

8. **Gumroad**: This platform simplifies the selling of digital products, making it easier for solo founders to monetize their applications while integrating licensing features from auth-licence.
