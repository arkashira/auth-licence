# Customer Journey Map for auth-licence

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Solo founders or small SaaS developers encounter issues with piracy or unauthorized access to their web applications.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available solutions, overwhelming options, and complexity of existing products.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, anxiety about security and revenue loss.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blogs, webinars) explaining the importance of authentication and licensing; clear messaging on how auth-licence solves these issues.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users actively search for authentication and licensing solutions after recognizing their need.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty comparing features, pricing, and integration processes of various solutions.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, cautious optimism, and hope for finding a suitable solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a comparison tool, customer testimonials, and case studies showcasing successful integrations.
- **Metric**: Number of product comparisons made and inquiries received.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to test auth-licence through a free trial or demo.
- **Friction Points**: Complicated onboarding process, unclear documentation, and potential technical issues during integration.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension about the product's performance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding process, responsive customer support, and comprehensive guides/tutorials.
- **Metric**: Trial sign-up rates and user feedback during the trial period.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully integrate auth-licence into their applications and start using it regularly.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup challenges, ongoing maintenance, and potential bugs.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction upon successful integration, but possible frustration with ongoing issues.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular check-ins from customer success teams, proactive support for any issues, and user community forums for sharing experiences.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription and customer satisfaction scores.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users experience success with auth-licence and consider upgrading or adding features.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the value of additional features, potential costs, and integration with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the product, but cautious about additional investments.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized recommendations for upgrades, showcasing ROI from existing features, and offering loyalty discounts.
- **Metric**: Rate of feature adoption and upsell conversion rates.
